This School was started with primary classes. Later we opened the Junior Section. After that we got the permission from CBSE to start the Secondary Section in the year 1996. In 1998, CBSE granted us affiliation to upgrade the School up to the Senior Secondary Level for Science,Humanities and Commerce subjects and the classes are going on under the Science stream. We aim to achieve higher goals in the field of education by following internationally recognized pedagogical techniques and make innovation. The School emphasizes on interactive and child centered education.Keeping this in mind we have a system of flexible syllabus in order to suit the pace of learning of the students and we lessen the burden of home work. The aim of the school is to provide proper and best education to the children. The School imparts education in Primary, Junior, Secondary and Senior Secondary level with the aid of latest scientific apparatus and teaching aids. The school provides education under the guidance and control of a highly qualified staff. Computer Eduacation is also provided from Class-I onwards. We help our students to develop their personality on progressive lines with a comprehensive mental outlook and विद्ययामृतमश्नुते (vidyayamrtamasnute) self-confidence based on sound character and good conduct to face the future. These include English Week, Hindi Week and the celebration of important festivals. Inter-house activities include recitation, slogan-writing, essay writing, poster-making, calligraphy, drawing and quiz competitions. Inter-house competitions in games like volleyball, basketball, and kho-kho are held. Mass P.T, outdoor and indoor games are provided also Students have participated in interscholastic contests like, Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton etc.
The School is located on Gorakhpur- Sonauli Road, near Gorakhnath temple. The atmosphere is calm, peaceful and inspiring. It occupies an extensive park like area of about 4.00 acres. It is well planned with well ventilated, spacious class rooms and amenities.
