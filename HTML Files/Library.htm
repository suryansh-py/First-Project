LIBRARY

 "Knowledge is Power". We should add to our knowledge by library books.One of the best things in our school is LIBRARY.Its a huge and spacious library.Reading rooms are its branch.There are about 10,000 books on different topic/subjects.We also have a book issue system.Books can be issued by students for reading it at home,for a week.Our library has updated newspapers and magzines.For the safety of books we have installed cameras.Our library is divided in three sections :-
  • CHILDREN SECTION
    This section has the books for the students of class Nursery to Class III.This section has animated books,3-D books,playbooks and many toys and puzzle for the kids.Kids come here and enjoy a lot as they come to know about many stories,jokes and they also learn good habits.
  • SECONDARY SECTION
    We also have a section for the students of class IV to VIII and a section for growing students should be full of knowledgable books.So this consists of updated magzines and newspapers,dictionaries,fictional books,biographies,reasoningand the books which can improve the mental and thinking ability of a student.This section also consists of books related to student's curriculum.Students can sit in the library and clearify their doubts with the deep study of any topic.
  • SENIOR SECTION
    This section is alloted for the students of class IX to XII.This section mainly consists of books related to the student's academic studies.Magzines,novels,biographies,many famous autobiographies and many motivational books which can give a good shape to carrer of a student.Recently our school also installed e-learning in which students can have deep and thorogh knowledge on a particular topic with the help of computers.Seats alotted to the students are according to their roll no. and are arranged in a manner that no one can disturb the others.
    "OUR SCHOOL'S LIBRARY IS THE REAL TEMPLE OF LEARNING."
