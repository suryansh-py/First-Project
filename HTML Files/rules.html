  1. Students must come to school before the first assembly bell at 8:55 a.m.The school gate will be closed at 9:00 a.m.Late commers won't be allowed to enter the school.
  2. Students are not allowed to come to school by riding scooter or moped.
  3. Lunch Box:The students shall carry their Lunch Boxes along with them.Lunch box shall not be accepted by the office for passing on the same to the particular student under any circumstances.
  4. Keep a sense of self-respect in your dress and behaviour.
  5. The hairs of the girls should be cut small,long hairs should be tied in double pleats with black ribbon.coloured pins,coloured ribbons and coloured hairbands are not allowed.
  6. Girls are not allowed to use any gold and silver ornaments.School will not be responsible if any costly item is lost.
  7. Be simple,polite and obliging in all your dealings with others.
  8. Make use of the words 'please' and 'thanks' more often avoid using indecent language.Respect your elders and teachers,stand-up whenever you see them.You should be polite and well behaved in the school compound and outsides.
  9. Students should not make a noise in the class as well as in the school compound to maintain the discipline.
  10. Students must not walk about in the verandah and around the class-room during the class hours.
  11. Absentees won't be allowed to enter the class without the permission of the Principal.Leave application with parent's signature must be submitted in the Principal's office.
  12. Absolute silence must be observed during the assembly,and while moving in the class.
  13. Students should ot write or scratch on the school walls or deface the school furniture.
  14. Students must not throw paper or food stuffs anywhere in school premises except in the trashcan/dustbin provided for purpose.
  15. Pupils are not allowed to enter the staff-rooms.
  16. During class hours students will not be allowed to go home or else where.In case of some emergent cases,the parents of the student has to give a written application for short leave.However the decision will be at the discretion of the Principal as per the importance of the subject.
  17. White uniform is a must on Saturday.
  18. To obtain fluency in English,English should be spoken by the students in and around the school.
