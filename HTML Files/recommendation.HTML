Recommendations To Parents
  1. Parents are expected to co-operate with the school authorities,by ensuring that the children observe puntuality and discipline,school uniform, prepare the lessons and take active part in the activites of the school
  2. Parents should check the school diary and sign the reports recorded by the teacher and homework given
  3. Parents/gaurdians are requested to keep themselves in touch with the school to have knowledge of their ward,s progress in studies
  4. Parents are not allowed to meet the teachers in the classroom or in the staff-room. Those who wish to seek information ,suggestion or make complaint about their children's education or other matters may meet the principal between 9:30 to 11:30a.m
  5. We have come across that a few students often come late. Inspite of instruction they do not change the habit of late coming. This is not a healthy practice either for the students or gaurdians who donot take note of it . Therefore, it is specifically brought in this diary that any students coming late shall look into this bad habits of late coming by their wards and ask them to reach school within time
    6. We wish that the students of class 6 and above must come to school in school uniform on their birthday also however ,students of lower classes may come to school on their birth days in clean and decent home dress
  6. School authorities will be responsible for the students only during school hours
  7. The parents shall ensure that their wards studying upto class 5 do not come to the school by riding bicycles
  8. The parentsgaurdians are requested to provide their mobile number ,e-mail as well as home address to the class teacher in the event of any change of the same
