Our school firmly believe in child-centred education and attempt to offer a unique opportunity for the optimum development of children by using the aid of latest psychological and scientific apparatus and teaching aids.The school seeks excellence in the field of learning and offers the latest audio-visual aids and provides education equipments to create a suitable atmosphere for the all round development of children .

The school makes speial arrangements for extra coaching to the students who are found weak in a certain subject.An effort is made to pay individual attention to all students in academic as well as in their physical,mental and moral developments.Jayanties of the National Heros are celebrated on the ocassion of their anniversaries.

  • DISCIPLINE

Students are expected to maintain a high standared of good manners,behaviour and etiquette.If a child is found involved in any undesired activity or exhibiting an offensive attitude,she/he will be expelled from the school on charges of indiscipline.

  • MORNING ASSEMBLY

Morning assembly is held everyday,followed by "Thought for the Day"and "News"read by students and moral teaching by teachers.

Regular P.T.is held.A breif time is also alloted to increase th general knowledge of our students.

  • CO-CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES AND S.U.P.W.

The school offers a variety of hobbies and actvities.Every student is supposed to take part and co-operate in the various school activities such as debateing,delivering speech,essay writing,quiz etc.Additional arrangements for Music,Art,Dance,Model Making,clay work,Pottery,Gardening,stitching and embroidery etc have been made for the students.these activities ultimately shape the balanced personality in an individual whin switching from adolescence to adulthood.

Our English and Hindi Literary Societies organise Debate,Declamation test,Quiz omprtition,Essay writing and Handwriting competition.The Cultural Society organises competition in Dramatics,Painting andMusic

  • RELATION BETWEEN PARENTS AND CHILDREN
  1. Harsh and coercive means of punishment should not be used in disciplining the children.They are to be trained throgh love and effection.
  2. Help your children to set their own individual goals in their lifes and assist them to attain excellence in all the undertaking.
  3. Do not allow your children to play out on the street with other kids and be very careful about the companion they keep.
    • Homework is given everyday.Parents are requested to see that their children complete their homework regularly.
    • Parents Teachers Meeting are periodically organised in which discussion is done over child's education and welfare matters.
