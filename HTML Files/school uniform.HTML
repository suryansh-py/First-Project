SCHOOL UNIFORM

SUMMER UNIFORM:

a) BOYS:
  1. Half sleeved sky blue shirt and Navy blue half pants,from Nursery to V.
  2. Full sleeved sky blue shirt and Navy Blue Full Pants from VI onwards.
b) GIRLS:
  1. Half sleeved sky blue blouse and Navy blue Tunic (Nursery to V).
  2. Navy blue skirt and Full sleeved sky blue blouse from VI onwards.
  • Black shoes and Navy blue socks.
  • Black ribbon/Black hair band.
  • Belt with Monogram and Tie

NRY TO V :

Red Blazer, V-neck Sweater and trousers (For Girls and Boys)

VI to XII

Navy Blue Blazer Sweater and trousers (For Girls and Boys)

  1. Half sleeved white shirt and white half pants from Nursery to V.

ATTENDANCE

  1. Students must be punctual and regular in attendance. Leave application must be submitted to the Principal's office. In case of illness and other unforeseen circumstances, the guardian should inform the principal in writing within two days. He/She receiving treatment from a doctor must submit photocopy of the prescription alongwith the original which after perusal will be returned. A minimum attendance of 90% of the total working days is required during the academic year. A student returning to school after suffering from an infection or contagious disease, should produce a doctor's certificate, permitting him/her to attend the school.

  2. Every student must be present on the re-opening day after each vacation.

  3. No leave other than Medical leave will be sanctioned during the examination.

  4. Attendance is compulsary, on National Festivals such as the "Independence Day","Republic Day" etc.Absence on these days will be punishable.
