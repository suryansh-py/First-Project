LABS

ART ROOM

Our school have a large room specially for Art and Craft activities.The whole room is full of imaginative craft works and it also increases the imagination level of a student.Our school have appointed the speacialist teachers for these work,they also guide students in doing all these activities.The school's art room is a huge place and have almost 90 seats for the students.Students also show a keen interest towards these activities.It is faciliated by three air-conditioners.Each class has one period for Art in a week.School also conducts summer camps in which activities like art &craft and clay art is a big category.In summer vacations students come and learn craft works,it also helps in relaxing their minds.In Clay Art,students learn to make toys by shaping the clay.

SST LAB

Our school also have a Social Science Lab on the third(3th)floor which is a spacious room having interesting stuffs related to social science.Its a beatiful place and useful one too.The social sceience often take students to the lab to explain the exact location of a particular site on a large maps.Students also are interested in such type of explaination.It also increases the interest level of student in social science.The lab also consists of 3-D maps,3-D models of various historical buildings,charts on the status of various countries, models of ancient currencies of various countries and it also consists of historical statues related to our culture and chapters of our textbooks.Upto a large extent it converts a tough subject in an interesting one,which we generally do not see around.Students enjoy being here.

COMPUTER LABS

Our school has two Computer labs which are huge and spacious and is situated at fisrt(1st)floor.These labs are used to perform practicals or to explain the computer topics thoroghly.Computers,in both the labs,are of first class and it works very smoothly as it has appropriate amount of RAM(Random Access Memory),memory and a good-fast processor,which is required for the students for doing work.Good and helpful softwares are their to help the students.Hardwares present here works smoothly and are in good conditions to use.Electricity cases are also good and efficient in these cases.It also has air-conditioners which regulates the temperature.School appoints high class teachers which makes students understand a topic.Cameras are installed in these labs for the safety of the computers and its parts.

CHEMISTRY LABS

Our school has two Chemistry labs,one at the ground floor and one at first floor.Its huge and spacious and has the sufficient number of seats for the students.Various kinds of chemicals are present and are always in stock/available.It also has Periodic table from first one to the mordern one.Equipments such as tri-pod stand,flask,petridish,testtubes and testtube stands are also available in the lab.Washbasins for cleanliness of the hands after experiments are also available.All the experiments are performed under the supervision of expert teachers.Teachers also guide the students in performing the experimnts.Students first keenly observe the expriments perfomed by the teachers,and then perform it on their own.In case of a fire due to failure of an experiment,fire extinguisher are available for their services.
